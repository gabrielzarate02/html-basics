Chipmunk Soul

Category: Hip Hop

In terms of sound, there was a clear correlation between backpack and TriBeCa music scenes characterized by the use of high-pitched, whimsical soul samples known as "chipmunk soul." This particular style gained immense popularity during the first to middle years of the 2000s.

Genres

Hip Hop

Hip hop is a cultural movement and music genre that originated in African American and Latino communities in the Bronx, New York City in the 1970s. It is characterized by a rhythmic and rhyming speech that is accompanied by DJ scratching, beatboxing, and music production.
